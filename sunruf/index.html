What is solar energy?

Solar Energy is a form of energy from the Sun that is converted into electric & thermal energy. We have been using this energy for so many years but for different purposes like heating, cooking & drying.

What are the environmental benefits of solar energy?

Solar power, like other renewable energy resources, has many environmental and health benefits. Going solar reduces greenhouse gas emissions, which contribute to climate change, and also results in fewer air pollutants like sulfur dioxide and particulate matter, which can cause health problems.

Is solar energy safe to use?

Of Course it is very safe to use. It doesn’t produce any harmful effect while using it. It is more like the electricity you are using currently for carrying out your daily chores.

Can I afford to go solar?

Though you are thinking using solar may cost you more than your electricity bills or it’s just a Rich people thing, it’s not like that it is very much affordable to all. Once you install a solar panel or solar system for your home, you don’t need to worry about at least the next 25 years. They are so affordable. Still you are thinking that your budget or solar system is out of your reach. They are options like finance & Govt. Subsidies are available. You can take advantage of it.

What are my solar financing options?

There are two solar financing options: you can purchase your system in cash or take out a solar loan to buy your system,its calculations are based on your roof plus real quote data from your area.

Can I get the subsidy from the Govt if I install solar?

Yes, some states in India do have subsidy schemes and some offer to buy the extra solar electricity that you make (through grid-tie systems). You can check more details about solar subsidy on the official website of MNRE of India. However, in reality, very few people are able to avail of subsidies because it requires too much paperwork and too much time. Our advice would be to forget about subsidies and select a solar system that gives you a good ROI. We have seen that some people recover the cost of installation in just 5 years!

Is my roof suitable for solar panels?

Solar panels work best with durable, strong roofing material. If you have wooden shake & slate tiles or roof which is made up of clay with mortar or any metal/stone coated roof then also you can go for solar panels. But to install it you need a professional like SunRuf, who has experience in working with these types of roofs.

What are the different types of solar panels?

There are 3 different types of Solar panels. They are as follows -
1.   Monocrystalline Solar Panels -
These are the oldest types of Solar panels & the most developed ones. These monocrystalline Solar Panels are made of 40 solar monocrystalline cells. These are square shaped cells. They are made up of silicon. When you see it you find it black as sunlight reacts with the pure silicon.
2.   Polycrystalline Solar Panels -
These solar panels are newer in development but due to popularity & efficiency they are rising quickly. They are also made up of silicon, same as monocrystalline solar panels but the difference is that they are made up of fragments of silicon crystals melted together. You find them blue as the sunlight reacts with them. They are also square in shape but without gaps.
3.   Thin-Film Solar Panels -
An extremely new development of solar panels. They are not always made up of silicon, other materials like Cadmium Telluride, Amorphous Silicon, Copper Indium Gallium Selenide also can be used to make these solar panels. These panels are created by placing main material between thin sheets conducting material & with the glass layer on the top of it for protection.

Do I need to install solar batteries with my solar power system?

As Solar energy is a renewable source of energy, it’s storage is very important. You can use stored energy whenever there is an electricity outage in your area. Though these batteries are high cost & low efficiency, they can be used mostly in the off-grid Solar systems.

How long will my solar power system last?

Your solar power system lasts till 25 years. The solar systems which were installed in the 1980s are still working fine to produce energy. Solar panels are very reliable & durable.

After installing the solar system do I need to worry about maintenance and service?

Solar panels are quite rough and tough and come with a warranty of 25 years. However, with time the solar power system (panels, inverter and battery) may get some technical problems. So it is very important that you buy your solar system from a reputed company like SunRuf who offers good service as well. We discourage you from buying cheap Chinese solar products because they are very unreliable. When you are installing solar, think of it as an investment. A good investment will bring great returns. Always go with a reliable company.

How do I find out how much I pay for electricity?

The easiest way to find out how much you pay for electricity (and how much electricity you use per month) is to take a look at your utility electricity bill. Review Sunruf guide to reading your electricity bill to find out exactly what to look for.

What is net metering?

Net metering is the system that utilities use to credit solar energy system owners for the electricity produced by their solar panels. With net metering, you only pay for the electricity that you use beyond what your solar panels can generate. Net metering policies differ from state to state.
